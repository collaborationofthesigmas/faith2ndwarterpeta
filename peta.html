About Us

Faith Christian School is dedicated to providing quality
education grounded in biblical principles. Our goal is to
inspire students to pursue academic excellence and
grow in their faith.

Our mission is to equip students with knowledge and character through Christ-centered education.

Vision

We envision a community where every student grows in faith, knowledge, and service to others.
